# ZedApply refund policy

**Effective date:** To be set at publish time  
**Last updated:** 2026-05-23  
**Contact:** Bwana chat → type **talk to human** for billing disputes, or email [convergeozambia@gmail.com](mailto:convergeozambia@gmail.com)

This policy applies to subscription fees paid through ZedApply’s payment partners
(Lenco mobile money, DPO Pay cards). All amounts are in Zambian Kwacha (ZMW).

---

## 1. Overview

We want you to be confident upgrading. Refunds are handled fairly and consistently
with the rules below. This page supplements (and, once published at `/legal/refunds`,
supersedes conflicting wording in) our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

---

## 2. Seven-day no-questions refund (no AI documents used)

If you upgrade to a paid tier and **have not generated any AI documents** on your
account (including tailored CVs, cover letters, or other AI-written application
materials), you may request a **full refund within 7 calendar days** of the charge.

No detailed justification is required for eligible requests in this window.

**AI documents used** means any successful generation recorded against your user ID
after the payment date. If our systems show AI document activity, this section does
not apply — see §4.

---

## 3. Tier downgrade within the billing cycle

If you **downgrade** to a lower paid tier or to Free **during an active billing
period** (before renewal), you may receive a **prorated refund** for the unused
portion of the higher tier, at our discretion and subject to payment-processor
constraints.

Proration is calculated from the downgrade date to the end of the current paid
period. Mobile-money refunds may take 3–10 business days to appear on your wallet.

Downgrading does not automatically trigger a refund — you must request one via
§6.

---

## 4. Non-refundable when AI documents were generated

Subscription fees are **non-refundable** once you have **generated AI documents**
on a paid tier during the billing period in question. This includes, without
limitation:

- AI-generated tailored CVs
- AI-generated cover letters
- Other AI-written application or interview materials delivered through ZedApply

This rule applies even if you are within 7 days of payment, because the paid
feature (AI generation) has been consumed.

---

## 5. Renewals and repeat refunds

- Automatic **renewals** are generally non-refundable once the new period starts.
- Accounts that have already received a refund under §2 may not claim another
  7-day no-questions refund on a subsequent plan.
- We may make exceptions where the Service was materially unavailable — contact us
  under §6.

---

## 6. How to request a refund or dispute a charge

1. **In-app (preferred):** open **Bwana** chat and type **talk to human**. Describe
   your phone number (+260…), the charge date, and whether you used AI documents.
2. **Email:** [convergeozambia@gmail.com](mailto:convergeozambia@gmail.com) with the
   same details.

We aim to respond within **2 business days**. Approved refunds are processed back to
the original payment method where the processor allows; otherwise we will agree an
alternative (e.g. mobile-money reversal).

---

## 7. Cancellations vs refunds

**Cancellation** stops future charges; it does not by itself refund the current
period. You retain paid access until the end of the period you already paid for.
See [Terms §5](/legal/terms) for cancellation mechanics.

---

## 8. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or mobile-money
provider. Chargebacks for legitimately delivered paid features may result in
account suspension pending investigation.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted at
`/legal/refunds` with an updated effective date. Continued use of paid tiers after
the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
